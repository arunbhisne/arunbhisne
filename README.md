### What I'm building

**AI Education Platform** — Currently designing the interaction model for an 
AI-powered adaptive K–12 learning system. The hard problems: guardrail definition, 
confidence signal design, and the teacher-oversight layer that keeps a human 
meaningfully in control when the AI acts on a child's behalf.  
→ Case study shipping May 2026 at [arunbhisne.com](https://arunbhisne.com)

---

### What I think about

How humans stay in meaningful control when AI systems act on their behalf.  
Specifically: trust signals, intervention gates, failure recovery, and the design 
of the moment when an agent should ask instead of act.
